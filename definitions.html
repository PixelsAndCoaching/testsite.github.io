Definitions

Sometimes language on workouts can seem extremely foreign. Here's a list of commonly used terms in my workouts.

Build

Build: start slow, finish fast.

Fly

Butterfly

BK

Backstroke

BR

Breaststroke

FR

Freestyle

IM

Equal parts of each stroke in this order: Butterfly, Backstroke, Breaststroke, and Freestyle
Example: 200 IM = 50 Butterfly, 50 Backstroke, 50 Breaststroke, and 50 Freestyle
100 IM = 25 Butterfly, 25 Backstroke, 25 Breaststroke, and 25 Freestyle

IM Switchers

Swim half the distance of one stroke, then the next stroke in IM order for the second half.
Example: 4x50 IM switchers
1. 50 (25 Butterfly, 25 Backstroke)
2. 50 (25 Backstroke, 25 Breaststroke)
3. 50 (25 Breaststroke, 25 Freestyle)
4. 50 (25 Freestyle, 25 Butterfly)

Moderate

this is an aerobic pace that you would be able to sustain for about thirty minutes.

Fast vs. Sprint

Fast pace is like a lactic acid threshold or solid effort, where as sprint means give it everything you've got.

DPS

Distance Per Stroke. Count the number of strokes it takes to complete a length.

Ez

Easy

Pull

Use a pullbouy between your legs or swim with only your arms.

Masters Minute

Take a quick break during a swim set. Chill for exactly 60 seconds, or as long as you think a minute is.
